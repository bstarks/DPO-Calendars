Print - Click to open the print dialogue.

Go To Year - Enter the desired year in the text box in front of the Go To Year button and then click on the Go To Year button to start the calendar on the desired year.

Previous - Click to start the calendar two years before the year currently shown on the calendar. Each click steps two more years into the past.

Current - Click to restore the displayed caledar to the current year.

Next - Click to start the calendar two years farther into the future than the year currently shown on the calendar. Each click steps two more years into the future.

View - Click to select which items to display with the calendar.

Help - Click to see or hide this help dialogue.
